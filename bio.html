Meet Melynda

Hi, I'm Melynda (also known as Mindy.) I started my career path in mechanical engineering and quickly realized that traditional engineering jobs lacked a human touch. Sure, I love working with computers and machines, but I enjoy being around people so much more. That's when I discovered UX and human factors!

Now, I'm a master's student in Mechanical Enigneering and Human and Computer Interaction at Iowa State University, where I do research at the Virtual Reality Applications Center. The majority of my research here invloves the evaluation of Augmented Reality and Virtual Reality systems. I enjoy the challenge of designing and evaluating 3D interfaces in AR and VR because they are areas of UX that are new and challenging.

My goal is simple, to make users a part of the conversation and build products that make their lives easier, less chaotic, and more fulfilling. Through my experiences in UX and human factors, I have learned a variety of skills in user research, human centered design, and human-computer interaction. But my greatest asset is a love for learning. I love learning new design methods to make products better. In fact, I've got a long list of reading to do!

My other great love is travel. I love seeing new places and learning about people. How they work. How they think. How they live. So it's no wonder that I've developed a passion for user research and interaction design. Some of my dream destinations include Machu Picchu and Cape Town.
