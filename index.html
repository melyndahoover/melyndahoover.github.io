About Me

I am an inquisitive UX Researcher with a passion for learning. I am currently pursuing my PhD in Human Computer Interaction at Iowa State University, where I conduct research in virtual and augmented reality. I enjoy the challenge of evaluating 3D interfaces in AR and VR because they are applications that pose new and challenging UX problems.

When I'm not working, I spend my time baking, training for half-marathons, and traveling the world with my husband as often as I can. I hope to keep learning and honing my craft to make better experiences for people around the world.
